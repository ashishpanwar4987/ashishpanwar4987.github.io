About

AP

I am a Marine Chief Engineer by profession and training. I spent years at sea running ships' engine rooms — the machinery spaces where fuel, steel, and heat are turned into motion — before coming ashore into classification. Today I serve as Principal Surveyor and Area Manager (South East Asia) for the Indian Register of Shipping, an IACS member society, based in Singapore.

My work sits at a rare intersection. Shipping must decarbonise, and I believe nuclear propulsion — small modular reactors at sea — is the only answer that survives the arithmetic. At the same time, artificial intelligence is reshaping how ships are surveyed, inspected, and operated. The regulatory frameworks for both are being written right now, and I write about them from the surveyor's vantage: the person who has to sign the certificate.

Everything I publish across LinkedIn, Medium, and Substack is catalogued here.
